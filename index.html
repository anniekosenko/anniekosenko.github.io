Who I Am

Born in Mykolayiv, Ukraine, in 1996, I grew up in a culturally diverse family with a Russian father and a Ukrainian mother. Drawing has been my passion since childhood, and I honed my skills during two years of art school. At 13, I moved to Spain, where I embraced a new culture that expanded my perspective and enriched my creativity. Traveling and visiting museums, exhibitions, and galleries continue to inspire my work and fuel my artistic journey.

My Work

My work is rich with color, contrast, and energy, designed to captivate and inspire. Each piece invites viewers to immerse themselves and discover their own meaning, as I believe art truly comes to life in the eyes of those who experience it.
